9 GORGEOUS PLACES TO STAY NEAR MUMBAI AND PUNE



From private-pool villas to heritage bungalows, these are your best bets at a weekend getaway from Mumbai and Pune





Update: 9th November





  • Amanzi,Pawna
  • Le Meridian Mahabaleshwar Resort & Spa
  • Jal Srushti,Mulshi
  • Atmantan wellness Resort,Mulshi
  • Le Farm, Kamshet
  • By the Beach,Alibaug
  • Dune Barr House - Verandah in the Forest, Matheran
  • Casa Blanca,Karjat
  • The Machan Resort, Lonavala

Amanzi,Pawna

Whether its adventure or solitude you're seeking, Amanzi has it all. Since it is located in Tikona, a two-hour drive from Mumbai and an hour from Pune, a bunch of activities such as trekking, paragliding and kayaking are on the menu. But the property that overlooks the Pawna dam also offers the perfect place to just sit back and relax. From a Garden Villa to a Cocoon Villa shaped like a dome, there are plenty of stay options to soak in the beauty. But for ultimate relaxation, choose the Glass Treehouse Suite that offers sweeping views of the Sahyadris and the lake. The two-bedroom accommodation boasts a spacious living room and a plunge pool. The restaurant Arches takes you on a culinary adventure around India, with platters from the Hindu Kush mountains to food that dates back to the Mandwa dynasty.

Distance:122km from Mumbai; 66km km from Pune.
Tariff: Doubles from Rs10,975 for the Garden Villa.
Visit Amanzi









Le Meridian Mahabaleshwar Resort & Spa

In a hill station that swarms with tourists, no matter what part of the year, it's a blessing to have a luxe hotel that assures some degree of privacy—rooms are housed in stand-alone buildings separated by large areas of woods. The suites, some with terraces, look out to the hills and their evergreen forests. When the rains come down and paint the surroundings green, the place becomes a perfect base to stare out at them all while you sip on some strawberry tea. A kids' club and a library keep your little ones busy, while you have the rooftop pool and the spa for a blissful time.

Distance: 230km from Mumbai; 121km from Pune.
Tariff: Doubles from Rs13,775.
Visit Le Meridian









Jal Srushti,Mulshi

Just before River Mula meets Mulshi lake, it rings around a spot of land on which the Jal Srushti Island Resort stands. Pick from the riverside or pondside cottages—they're all wood and glass, with balconies over the water. Mornings usually start with birdsong, but it's the sitar recitals that steal the show. A strawberry garden and a riverside restaurant complete the pretty picture.

Distance: 167km from Mumbai;37km from Pune.
Tariff: Doubles from Rs11,000.
visit Jal Srushti









Atmantan wellness Resort,Mulshi

There are weekends when just splashing about in the pool isn't going to do the trick. That's when you head to this 40-acre wellness retreat overlooking Mulshi lake. The 106-room resort features guest rooms and villas dotted across different heights on the hill. The fitness and spa equipment is state-of-the-art, and the meals are fresh and healthy (but thankfully not strictly vegetarian.

Distance: 172km from Mumbai; 48km from Pune.
Tariff: Singles from Rs75,000 for minimum 3 nights.
Visit Atmatan









Le Farm, Kamshet

If you like your mornings cool, windy and with views of lakes, exit the expressway near Lonavala and drive past a village or three to arrive at this stylish villa. The minimalist décor with clean lines and large windows gives this property a relaxed, airy feel. Le Farm can host a party of up to 30 guests, perfect for that big family getaway you've been putting off for long. The paragliding schools of Kamshet are a short drive away for those who are seeking a real high.
Distance: 114km from Mumbai; 60km from Pune.
Tariff: From Rs39,000 for up to 12 guests.
Visit Le Farm









By the Beach,Alibaug

Just a five-minute drive from Mandwa jetty lies this luxurious five-bedroom villa outfitted with intricately carved, vintage-style furniture, day beds, four-poster beds and rattan furniture outdoors. Read a book on the lawns between dips in the pool, play a game of table tennis or enjoy a movie at the mini-theatre.

Distance: 100km from Mumbai; 143km from Pune.
Tariff: 2,53,700 for 10 adults, inclusive of meals.
Visit By the Beach









Dune Barr House - Verandah in the Forest, Matheran

Exit the Expressway at Karjat and drive to Dasturi village at the foot of the Sahyadris. Here, park your car, lace up your shoes and march up the mountain for an hour to reach this charming 11-room heritage property. Built in the 19th century, Verandah in the Forest comes with all colonial charms. Its location keeps you away from the bustle of the market but puts you straight in monkey territory. Fair deal, some say, till their reading glasses end up on the tree.

Distance: (Till Dasturi village) 100km from Mumbai; 120km from Pune.
Tariff: Doubles starting Rs5,500.
Visit Dune Barr House









Casa Blanca,Karjat

Every nook and corner of Casa Blanca exudes old-world charm. With Chesterfield sofas, walls handpainted with the Eiffel Tower and Mona Lisa and a vintage car that has been upcycled into a bar, the four-bedroom home will transport you to the streets of Paris. Spend your weekend sunbathing in the private pool or snuggling by the bonfire.

Distance: 64km from Mumbai;99km from Pune.
Tariff: Rs34,000 for the entire villa.
Visit Casa Blanca









The Machan Resort, Lonavala

With spectacular wood-and-glass decks hoisted on trees, The Machan gives you the most dramatic stay in the region. Pick from the several well-furnished machans for a weekend to remember. Those not comfortable with heights can opt for the cabins. Do note that the property runs mostly on solar power, so air-conditioning is not available. But the forest, we're told, keeps things cool. When it comes to eco-resorts in the region, you can see why this one's tough to top.

Distance: 100km from Mumbai; 82km from Pune.
Tariff: Doubles from Rs8,000 for the Cabin; Rs13,000 for the Jungle Machan.
Visit Machan
